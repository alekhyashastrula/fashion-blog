About Shastrula Studios

Welcome to Shastrula Studios — your destination for beautiful mul cotton sarees. We design lightweight, breathable sarees using the finest mul cotton fabric, perfect for the Indian woman who loves comfort without compromising on style.

From classic solids and hand block prints to elegant zari borders, every saree we create celebrates the beauty of Indian draping. Based in India, we bring the softness of mul cotton and the richness of Indian tradition straight to your doorstep.
