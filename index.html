Saree Details & Draping Guide

📏

Saree Dimensions

  • Length: 5.5 meters (standard)
  • Width: 44 inches
  • Blouse piece: 0.8 meters (unstitched)
  • Fabric: Pure mul cotton
🎀

Care Instructions

  • Dry clean for first wash
  • Gentle hand wash after that
  • Do not bleach
  • Iron on medium heat
  • Store folded in a cool, dry place
✌

Draping Tips

  • Mul cotton drapes beautifully without pins
  • Best for Nivi style draping
  • Lightweight — perfect for all-day wear
  • Pairs well with oxidised or gold jewellery
  • Great for office, festivals & casual outings

About Shastrula Studios

Frequently Asked Questions

What is mul cotton?

+

Mul cotton (also called mulmul) is an ultra-lightweight, breathable cotton fabric. It's softer than regular cotton and drapes beautifully, making it perfect for sarees — especially in Indian summers. It feels like a second skin!

Can I customise the colour of a saree?

+

Yes! All our sarees are available in custom colours. Just DM us on Instagram or mention your preferred colour while placing your order. We'll confirm the shade with you before making it.

How long does delivery take?

+

Orders within India are delivered in 7–12 business days. International orders take 15–25 business days. You'll receive a tracking number once your order is shipped.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept payments via UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.). After placing your order, you'll see a QR code to scan and pay. It's quick and secure!

Do you offer returns or refunds?

+

All sales are final — we don't offer returns or refunds. However, if you receive a damaged product, contact us within 24 hours with photos and we'll sort it out for you.

Does the saree come with a blouse piece?

+

Yes! Every saree comes with a 0.8-meter unstitched blouse piece in a matching colour.
