Meet the Maker

Hi, I'm Alekhya Shastrula, Founder of Shastrula Studios.

I grew up watching my mother drape sarees with effortless grace — and somewhere between those folds and pleats, I fell in love with the art of wearing one. Shastrula Studios was born from that love.

Every saree here is a piece of my heart — handpicked fabrics, colours chosen with intention, and craftsmanship that honours tradition while embracing the modern woman. I believe elegance shouldn't come with a heavy price tag, and beauty lies in simplicity.

My dream is simple: to make every woman feel like the most beautiful version of herself, one saree at a time.

— With love,
Alekhya Shastrula

Questions

What is mul cotton?

+

Mul cotton (also called mulmul) is an ultra-lightweight, breathable cotton fabric. It's softer than regular cotton and drapes beautifully, making it perfect for sarees — especially in Indian summers.

Can I customise the colour?

+

Yes! Choose any colour from our colour charts above. Only the shades shown are available. Pick your favourite, DM us the colour name on Instagram, and we'll dye it just for you.

How long does delivery take?

+

Orders within India are delivered in 7-12 business days. International orders take 15-25 business days.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept payments via UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.). After placing your order, you'll see a QR code to scan and pay.

Do you offer returns or refunds?

+

All sales are final — we don't offer returns or refunds. If you receive a damaged product, contact us within 24 hours with photos.

Does the saree come with a blouse piece?

+

Yes! Every saree comes with a 0.8-meter unstitched blouse piece in a matching colour.
